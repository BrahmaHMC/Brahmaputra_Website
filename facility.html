Hostel Library

Books are your friend

A library with more than 200 titles from various genres. And it's right here in your own hostel. From fiction to fantasy to biography, the library has a large collection. Make sure to give it a go!

Canteen

Source of food Anytime

The hostel has two canteens: one in each A and B blocks. From noodles to regular meals, a variety of items are served.

Cultural Room

Music and Masti

The cultural room has two guitars, one drum set and amplifier set for the various cultural events of the hostel.

Sports Room

Time to Play

Sports facilities include two table tennis tables in the sports room along with two carroms. The hostel also houses chess boards for the boarders to flex their minds. Badminton, cricket equipment, football, volleyball and all common sports equipment are available for boarders.

Juice Center

Juice center with varieties of juices

The hostel has 2 juice centres one in each A and B blocks where juices, shakes, fruit salad and other cold drinks are served.

TV Room

Let's Watch TV

The hostel has a large LCD TV along with Airtel Digital TV subscription so that boarders can enjoy their leisure time. We also have Sony Home theatre (5.1) for all the sound effects.

Mess Food

As the name says MESS with FOOD

Hostel offers various food on daily basis, a.k.a. Mess food. Maybe It won't Compare to home food but if you have any issues just contact Mess Convenver about anything

Adding to your Comfort

Brahmaputra offers above facilities to its boarder to make their experience more memorable.

About

Housing nearly thousand students, the hostel to its might is named aptly as Brahmaputra. The hostel began to function from academic year 2011-2012. It is boys hostel and boarders in the hostel are from various streams mainly pursuing their Ph.D, M.Tech, M.Sc, B.Tech.It is a three floored building with spacious rooms and all other facilities incorporated. The hostel is a centre for various activities.
